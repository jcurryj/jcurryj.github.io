Joshua Curry is a Philadelphia based musician. His credits include extensive recording and touring experience with all boy/all girl, Super Capsule, Futurist, Jack Killen and Modest Midas. Graduating in 2010, he holds a degree in instrumental performance from the University of the Arts.

Available for lessons in-person and on Skype. Subjects include; Beginner to advanced Bass Guitar, Double Bass, Guitar, Banjo, Composition, Improvisation, Ear training, Transcription, Sight reading, Music theory, Audition prep. Currently seeking commercial and film composition projects.
