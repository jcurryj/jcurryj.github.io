Joshua Curry is a Philadelphia based composer and multi-instrumentalist, though he keeps the bass nearby, always.
His credits include extensive recording and touring experience with;
all boy/all girl, Super Capsule, Futurist, Jack Killen and Modest Midas.
He holds a degree in Instrumental Performance from the University of the Arts.

Available for lessons in-person and on Skype. Subjects include;
Beginner to advanced Bass Guitar, Double Bass, Guitar, Banjo, Composition, Improvisation, Ear training,
Transcription and analysis, Sight reading, Music theory, Audition prep.

Currently seeking commercial and film composition projects.
